4th International Symposium on High Confidence Software (ISHCS 2015)

January 15, 2015, The Lakeview Hotel, Beijing, China

Host: Institute of Software and Key Laboratory of High Confidence Software Technologies, Ministry of Education, Peking University

Following great success of the first to the third International Symposium on High Confidence Software (ISHCS 2011, ISHCS 2012, ISHCS 2013), ISHCS 2015 is to provide a forum for researchers in related research areas to address the challenges in high confidence software, exchange ideas, and foster collaborations. The symposium is hosted and sponsored by Institute of Software and Key Laboratory of High Confidence Software Technologies, Ministry of Education, Peking University, Beijing. The symposium chair is Hong Mei, the Program Co-Chairs are Zhi Jin, Tao Xie and Lu Zhang. 

The symposium consists of a series of invited talks from internationally known researchers on the symposium topic and discussion/panel sessions to enable substantial interactions with attendees.
